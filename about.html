About Suresh:

Suresh is a Ph.D. Candidate at Rochester Institute of Technology's Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences , specializing in computational biology, machine learning, and computer vision. As a Graduate Research Assistant at KC-Lab, he develops advanced deep learning models to identify and analyze post-translational modifications in protein sequences, with a focus on natural language processing techniques and protein language models.

Suresh holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and an M.Sc. in Computer Systems and Knowledge Engineering from Tribhuvan University, Nepal. During his master’s program, he gained teaching experience as an instructor for courses in programming, database management systems, machine learning, and image processing.

Suresh’s professional experience includes working as a software engineer, contributing to health supply chain logistics systems, and participating in projects like mRNA codon optimization during his internship at Bayer AG.
