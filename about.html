About Suresh:

Suresh holds a Ph.D. in Computing and Information Sciences from Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences , Rochester Institute of Technology, specializing in AI/ML applications in computational biology. His research focuses on utilizing and developing protein language models and deep learning techniques to develop efficient models for protein downstream tasks, such as post-translational modification prediction. Suresh has collaborated on several biomedical research projects, including Long-COVID prediction, fall monitoring in older adults, and ECG classification using various deep learning techniques.

Suresh holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and an M.Sc. in Computer Systems and Knowledge Engineering from Tribhuvan University, Nepal. During his master’s, he conducted his thesis on the scale-invariant analysis of super-resolution images using various computer vision algorithms. He also has teaching experience as an instructor at multiple universities, teaching courses in programming, database management systems, AI, ML, and image processing.

Suresh’s professional experience includes working as a software engineer, contributing to health supply chain logistics systems ( mSupply), and worked in an mRNA codon optimization project during his internship at Bayer AG.
